FAQs

Why should I advertise all my new & used inventory on the Google Search Network?

Because car-buyers are using Google, to search for new, and used vehicles using specific search terms such as “2021 Kia Sportage” or “Used Mitsubishi Triton”, and when they are you should be putting your in-stock inventory in front of them. It is said that buying intent is highest when consumers are searching for information as they do on Google.

I am already advertising on Google how is Accelerate SEM different?

Managing Google campaigns is time consuming and labour intensive. It is almost impossible to create ads for every vehicle you have in stock and even harder to manage as new vehicles come into stock or existing vehicles are sold. Accelerate SEM automatically creates new ads for new in-stock vehicles and deletes old ones when any vehicle is sold.

How does Accelerate SEM work?

Accelerate SEM uses proprietary software to collect all your vehicle data and uses that data to create relevant keywords, ad copy, and unique landing page URL’s. The program also optimise’s each campaign which boosts the relevance score that Google assigns leading to lower Cost Per Click and Lower Advertising Costs.

What is meant by “Search Term”?

The “search term” is the phrase that is used by consumers when they are searching on Google. “New Hyundai i30” is a popular search term. If you have an in-stock vehicle that matches a car buyers search term you would want to have your ad in front of them. Right time, Right message, Right place.

How do you report?

You are set up with an easy to use dashboard which gives you access to live reporting, the standard template gives you all the important metrics including, search terms, cost per click, click thru rates, & total media spend etc. We license the program at rates that are extremely competitive and designed to provide complete transparency to all costs and associated media spend

How many dealers are using the program & how was it developed?

The Program was built and developed by a team of French data engineers and is currently being used by nearly 600 dealers in North America. It has been successfully tested, piloted and trialled in the Australian market. Cost Per Click is proving to be much lower than other standard Google auto campaigns and participating dealers are reporting increase in website enquiry, phone enquiry & sales. They are also reporting lower advertising costs, they are getting more for less.
